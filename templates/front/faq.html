Frequently Asked Questions

How can I create a check?

Want to create a check? It is very simple. Once you are logged in, click the green button labelled "Add Check". You have your check created!

How can I invite someone on my check?

Inviting someone or team member(s) to your check is premium feature. To the top right, click on your email to reveal a drop down. Select "Account Settings". Under "Team Access", upgrade your account by clicking "paid plans" link. Once you have subcribed to one of the plans, just click "Invite a Team Member". Add the team member's email and click send invite. The member will receive an invite and once accepted, will become a team member.

Can I give my check a better name and tags?

Yes you can! Just hover over the check and click the "unnamed" label to your left. On the modal window, add a suitable name and tags to your check.

How can I set period, grace and nag time?

Very simple! Under Period column on your check, click on the "1 day 1 hour 1 hour" label. A modal window with three sliders will pop. Use the sliders to set the Period time, Grace time and Nag Time as labelled.

Can I set when to receive periodic reports?

Yes! To the top right, click on your email to reveal a drop down. Select "Account Settings". Under Reports in Settings, select one of the options that suits you best. It is that simple!

What integrations can I add for my checks?

Integrations? Quite a number are available. On the Home page, click the Integrations tab to reveal six listed ones. Click on any of them and follow the guidance to set it up.

Where can I see my unresolved checks?

On the Home page, click the Issues tab to reveal unresolved checks. Easy, isn't it?

How can I delete a check?

To delete a check is simple. Just hover over the check to reveal a cog at the far right. Click on the cog and select the "Remove" option in the dropdown. A modal window will pop to ask you to confirm. If you are sure you want to delete the check, click the red button labelled "Remove". Remember that this action cannot be undone.

Can I pause monitoring my check?

Of course you can! To pause monitoring your check, hover over the check to reveal a cog at the far right. Click on the cog and select the "Pause monitoring" option in the dropdown.

Where can I get a report of my checks?

In case you want to see a summary of your checks' report, go to the top right and click on your email to reveal a drop down. Select "Check Reports". You will see your report there.
